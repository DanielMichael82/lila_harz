Care Guide

Sunlight

Avoid leaving your resin pieces in direct sunlight as long exposure to the products can discolour them.

Out of Reach

Please keep away your pieces away from & out of the reach of children.

Cleaning

To clean your pieces use a microfibre cloth and warm water but do not submerge in water. Do not use harsh cleaning product as this can alter the surface of the resin. Please do not place in the dishwasher or microwave.

Things to Consider

Due to the nature of resin bubbles may appear in your products, but this does not affect the quality of the products - embrace the imperfections .
As these products are handmade, no one product will be the same so please keep this in my mind when ordering. Products will be similar but not identical.
